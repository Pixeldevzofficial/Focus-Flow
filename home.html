Focus Flow

Focus Flow is a smart, web-based study planner designed to help students organize their time and stay on track with their academic goals. It lets users input their subjects, available study hours, and preferences, then instantly generates a clear, personalized timetable that can be downloaded as a PDF. With a clean interface and features like customizable themes and future AI-powered scheduling, Focus Flow makes planning effortless and helping students study smarter, not harder.
